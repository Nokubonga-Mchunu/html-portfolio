Nokubonga's Favorite Marvel Movies

My Top 3 movies of all-time

1. Spider-Man: No Way Home

This one in particular is my ultimate top favorite movie because I love All Spider-Man movies. The characters are also relatable to me because we share the same humor in certain scenes. However, simply put, I love it because it was my first childhood superhero movie I loved.

2. Iron Man

Iron man was the hero we never knew we needed until Avengers: Endgame. The movie is very interesting and has a lot of humour.

3. Barbie: Princess Charm School

This movie is an all time classic movie of all the Barbie movies for me. It is one of the first movies that taught me the importance of selflessness.
